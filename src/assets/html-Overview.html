Antenna and Sensor Technologies in Modern Medical Applications

A comprehensive review of the theoretical background, design, and the latest developments in the application of antenna technology. The book presents the most recent research in the burgeoning field of wireless medical telemetry and sensing that covers both wearable and implantable antenna and sensor technologies.
